Commoner

Medium humanoid, unaligned

The short name is typically one or two words used to refer to the creature in its special traits, actions, reactions, and legendary actions.


If the short name is left blank, the creature's full name will be used instead.


Examples of short names:

Full Name Short Name
Ancient Red Dragon dragon
Gelatinous Cube cube
Orc War Chief orc
Rust Monster rust monster

Check the proper noun box if this statblock describes a specific and unique individual or creature.


If this box is unchecked, the word "the" will precede the creature's short name/full name in its special traits, actions, reactions, and legendary actions. Any capitalization from the short name/full name will also be removed.


For example:

  • "Basilisk" becomes "the basilisk ..."
  • "Mind Flayer" becomes "the mind flayer ..."

Otherwise if the box is checked, the word "the" will not appear and capitalization will be preserved.


Examples of proper nouns:

  • "Casper the Friendly Ghost"
  • "Drizzt Do'urden"
  • "Lady Kima of Vord"
  • "Tiamat"
