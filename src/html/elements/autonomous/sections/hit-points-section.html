Hit Points

The creature's size determines its hit die size and hit die average:


Creature Size Hit Die Size Hit Die Average
Tiny d4 2.5
Small d6 3.5
Medium d8 4.5
Large d10 5.5
Huge d12 6.5
Gargantuan d20 10.5

The creature's hit points can be calculated using the following formula:

HP = (Hit Die Quantity * Hit Die Average) rounded down + CON HP where CON HP = Hit Die Quantity * CON Modifier

For more details, see "Hit Points" on page 7 of the D&D 5th Edition Monster Manual.
