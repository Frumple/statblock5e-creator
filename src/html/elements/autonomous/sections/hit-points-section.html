Hit Points

4 (1d8)

A creature's hit points can be calculated using the following formula:

HP = (Hit Die Quantity * Hit Die Average) rounded down + Constitution HP where Hit Die Average = (Hit Die Size / 2) + 0.5 and Constitution HP = (Hit Die Quantity * Constitution Modifier)
* See "Hit Points" on page 7 of the DnD 5e Monster Manual for full details.
